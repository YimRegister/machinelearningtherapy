Machine Learning Therapy

About


Machine learning algorithms can teach us a lot about ourselves as human beings. These writings explore different ways of using algorithmic metaphors to deepen our knowledge of ourselves, work through trauma and mental illness, and discover ways to rewrite our narratives about ourselves. Machine learning models learn from training data in order to try to make predictions in future scenarios. We will use different algorithmic techniques to foster our own growth and nurture our spirits as people in control of our own path. I've included a Welcome post to dedicate intentions for this resource, and to describe the intimate relationship between artificial intelligence and my own healing.
